The Perfect Hard Boiled Eggs

Description

Hard boiled eggs can be made in so many different ways. From runny insides for soups to hard, almost rubbery, over boiling.

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • pot
  • water
  • bowl
  • ice

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a rolling boil. The water should be able to easily cover the eggs whole.
  2. Place eggs in carefully and boil for 5 minutes.
  3. Prepare a bowl of ice adn water to place the eggs in and let cool.
  4. Peel eggs when ready to eat.
